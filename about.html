About

Lin's TV & Appliance has been in the Brazos Valley for over twenty years. We service a wide radius around our shop, up to 60 miles or more. We often do warranty services for major brands, such as Samsung, LG, Sears, Best Buy, and others. We proudly service all major TV and appliance brands.
