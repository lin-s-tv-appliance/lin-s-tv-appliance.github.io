Lin's TV & Appliance Service

Lin's TV & Appliance is dedicated to providing the best repair service available in the Brazos Valley and surrounding area. We service every kind of TV brand, including Samsung, LG, Mitsubishi, Vizio, and more. We have been in business for over twenty years and are proud to help our customers.

No appointments are necessary; come by any time on weekdays from 9-6. We can sometimes stay open on weekends by request.
